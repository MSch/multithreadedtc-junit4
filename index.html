Maven2 Repository

Add this snippet to your pom.xml file or your ~/.m2/settings.xml 

<repository>
  <id>multithreadedtc-junit4</id>
  <name>multithreadedtc-junit4</name>
  <url>http://msch.github.com/multithreadedtc-junit4/maven2</url>
</repository>
then in your pom.xml you can add multithreadedtc-junit4 as dependency: 
<dependency>
  <groupId>edu.umd.cs.mtc</groupId>
  <artifactId>multithreadedtc</artifactId>
  <version>1.01-junit4</version>
  <scope>test</scope>
</dependency>
